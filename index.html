    • Horus Alas

    Horus Alas is a writer, reporter, copyeditor and digital designer based in the Washington, D.C. area.

    His reporting covers national politics and policy, focusing on Maryland's congressional delegation, federal appropriations and the Supreme Court. He thrives reporting on these topics, as well as feature and enterprise stories highlighting the human element within larger events.

    As a designer, he aims to engage the user with streamlined features and interactive graphics that propel a story. Horus' pages provide a crisp backdrop for journalistic stories and product demos. His interactive graphics have tracked congressional candidates' fundraising, among other metrics.

    Horus has edited web content for NASA's Glenn Research Center in Cleveland, Ohio, and written for Capital News Service, the Diamondback and the Frederick News-Post.

    He will complete a master's degree in journalism at the University of Maryland in December 2019. Horus previously earned a bachelor's degree in philosophy from the same university, and is a native Spanish speaker with backgrounds in Latin, French and Italian.
